Analyst

Featured

For committed students ready to dive deep into quantitative finance through structured learning and projects.

Fellow

Premium

Elite tier for experienced members leading projects, mentoring others, and driving club initiatives.

Requirements

  • Analyst tier completion (or equivalent experience)
  • Demonstrated expertise in quantitative finance
  • Leadership in club activities or projects
  • Mentorship of junior members
  • Application and interview process
  • Commitment to 2+ semesters

Perks

  • All Analyst perks
  • Leadership opportunities
  • Mentorship program participation
  • Exclusive Fellow events
  • Research collaboration with faculty
  • Industry speaker access
  • Resume review and career guidance
  • Alumni network access
  • Committee leadership roles

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need prior finance experience?

No, but a strong foundation in mathematics and basic programming is preferred. Our curriculum is designed to build from fundamentals.

Is there a membership fee?

No, all membership tiers are free for University of Rochester students.

Can graduate students join?

Yes, we welcome both undergraduate and graduate students at all membership levels.

When does the next cohort start?

The Spring 2026 cohort begins February 6. Applications are accepted on a rolling basis.

What's the difference between Analyst and Fellow?

Analysts participate in the structured curriculum and projects. Fellows are experienced members who lead initiatives and mentor others.

Can I join multiple committees?

Yes, you can participate in multiple committees, though we recommend focusing on 1-2 to maximize your contribution.
