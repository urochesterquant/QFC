About the University of Rochester Quantitative Finance Club

Building the future of quantitative finance at the University of Rochester, one student at a time

Our Mission

The University of Rochester Quantitative Finance Club empowers students to excel in quantitative finance through rigorous education, hands-on competition, and professional development. We bridge the gap between academic theory and industry practice.

Our Vision

To become the premier student organization for quantitative finance education, producing graduates who are industry-ready and equipped with cutting-edge skills in algorithmic trading, risk management, and financial modeling.
