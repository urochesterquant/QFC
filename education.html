Spring 2026 Curriculum

A structured 10-week program covering essential quantitative finance topics

Week 1

Introduction to Quantitative Finance

Overview of quant finance, career paths, mathematical prerequisites

Week 2

Financial Markets & Instruments

Equity, fixed income, derivatives, market microstructure

Week 3

Time Series Analysis

ARIMA, GARCH, volatility modeling, stationarity

Week 4

Portfolio Theory & Optimization

Modern Portfolio Theory, Markowitz optimization, risk-return tradeoffs

Week 5

Options Pricing Models

Black-Scholes, binomial models, Greeks, volatility surfaces

Week 6

Risk Management

VaR, CVaR, stress testing, risk metrics

Week 7

Algorithmic Trading Strategies

Market making, statistical arbitrage, execution algorithms

Week 8

Machine Learning in Finance

Regression, classification, feature engineering, model validation

Week 9

Quantitative Research Methods

Factor models, alpha research, signal generation

Week 10

Capstone Project

Integration of all concepts, presentation skills
